**Woofer Care AI** is an ethical, AI-powered platform designed to promote responsible pet ownership through intelligent breed analysis and personalized care recommendations. By combining **Computer Vision** with **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**, we empower pet owners—especially adopters—with the knowledge they need to provide optimal care for their companions.

&gt; 🚫 **Ethical Commitment:** Woofer does not support, facilitate, or promote the buying, selling, or breeding of animals. We are dedicated to adoption support, welfare education, and responsible ownership.

---

## 🎯 Mission & Vision

### The Problem
* **Information Gap:** New pet owners, especially adopters, often lack breed-specific knowledge to provide proper care.
* **Health Risks:** Misunderstanding dietary, exercise, and grooming needs leads to preventable health issues.
* **Abandonment:** Lack of preparation and support contributes to pets being returned to shelters.
* **Unethical Markets:** Many platforms profit from pet sales, perpetuating puppy mills and irresponsible breeding.

### The Woofer Solution
1. **AI Breed Analysis:** Instant identification of breed characteristics to understand specific care needs.
2. **Personalized Care Guides:** RAG-powered recommendations for nutrition, exercise, grooming, and health monitoring.
3. **Health Tracking:** Tools to monitor vet appointments, exercise goals, and wellness milestones.
4. **Adoption Support:** Resources and education to promote successful adoptions and reduce returns.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **🔬 AI Breed Scanner:** Uses MobileNetV2 neural networks to identify dog breeds and provide instant care insights.
- **📋 Personalized Care Plans:** Tailored recommendations for nutrition, exercise, grooming, and health based on breed-specific needs.
- **📄 Care Guide PDFs:** Automated generation of comprehensive care guides for printing or sharing with vets.
- **🏥 Health Tracker:** Monitor vet appointments, exercise goals, grooming schedules, and health notes.
- **🛒 Smart Supply Recommendations:** Ethical affiliate links to pet supplies (no commission on live animals).
- **🏠 Adoption Resources:** Educational content promoting adoption and responsible ownership.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** [Streamlit](https://streamlit.io/) (Interactive data-rich UI)
* **AI/ML:** [TensorFlow](https://www.tensorflow.org/) & [Keras](https://keras.io/) (MobileNetV2 for breed identification)
* **RAG System:** Custom knowledge base with breed-specific care information
* **Backend Logic:** Python 3.9+
* **Database:** JSON (Persistent pet profiles and health records)
* **Document Generation:** [FPDF](http://www.fpdf.org/) (Personalized care guide PDFs)

## 🐾 How It Works

### Step 1: AI Analysis
Upload a photo of your dog, and our AI will analyze the breed characteristics with confidence scoring.

### Step 2: Get Personalized Recommendations
Receive instant, breed-specific guidance on:

- **Nutrition:** Diet type, calorie needs, feeding schedules, foods to avoid
- **Exercise:** Daily requirements, recommended activities, mental stimulation
- **Grooming:** Brushing frequency, bathing schedules, special care needs
- **Health:** Common issues to monitor, vet checkup schedules, lifespan expectations

### Step 3: Track & Shop
- Download personalized PDF care guides
- Track health milestones and vet appointments
- Find supplies through ethical affiliate partners (Biopet.az, Wolt, Tap.az)

## 💰 Ethical Business Model

Woofer Care AI operates on a sustainable, ethical revenue model that never involves transactions for live animals:

* **Freemium Model:** Basic breed analysis and care recommendations free for all users.
* **Premium Subscriptions:**
  - **Woofer Care Basic (4.99 AZN/mo):** Advanced health tracking, multiple pet profiles, priority support.
  - **Woofer Care Pro (9.99 AZN/mo):** Vet integration, detailed analytics, custom care plans.
* **Ethical Affiliate Marketing:** Commission on pet supplies, food, and accessories through partnerships with Biopet.az, Wolt Azerbaijan, and Tap.az. **We never earn from pet sales.**
* **Veterinary Partnerships:** Referral fees for connecting owners with trusted local vets.
* **Shelter Support:** Free premium access for registered animal shelters and rescue organizations.
