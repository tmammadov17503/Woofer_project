**Woofer** is a Fintech-driven marketplace designed to solve the "Trust Gap" in the pet industry. By combining **Computer Vision** for identity verification with a **Secure Escrow Payment System**, Woofer ensures that every pet transaction—from breeding fees to puppy sales—is safe, transparent, and verified.

---

## 🚀 The Problem & Our Solution

### The Problem
* **Market Fraud:** 70% of pet transactions in Azerbaijan occur on unverified social media platforms, leading to frequent deposit scams.
* **Unregulated Breeding:** Lack of identity verification leads to unethical practices and untraceable health histories.
* **Payment Risk:** Cash-based transactions offer no protection for buyers or sellers.

### The Woofer Solution
1. **AI Identity Verification:** Real-time breed and biometric analysis to create a unique **Pet-ID Token**.
2. **Fintech Escrow:** A secure "Safe-Hold" payment layer that protects funds until both parties verify the transaction.
3. **Automated Compliance:** Generation of official PDF receipts and digital audit trails for every deal.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **🛡️ AI Biometric Scanner:** Uses the MobileNetV2 neural network to identify dog breeds (e.g., Siberian Huskies) with high confidence.
- **💰 Smart Escrow:** Funds are locked in a virtual vault and only released upon mutual confirmation of pet health and identity.
- **📄 Instant PDF Receipts:** Automated generation of professional transaction records for legal and personal tracking.
- **📂 Persistent Registry:** A JSON-based decentralized database that stores pet history and verification tokens.

## 💰 Business Model
Woofer utilizes a diversified revenue strategy designed for high adoption and long-term financial sustainability in the Azerbaijani market. 

* **Freemium Matchmaking:** Core matchmaking features are accessible to all users to drive platform growth. 
* **Tiered Subscription Plans:** 
* **Woofer Basic (4.99 AZN/mo):** Includes verified profile badges and advanced search filters.
* **Woofer Pro (14.99 AZN/mo):** Targeted at professional breeders, providing official certifications.
* **Fintech Integration (Transaction Fees):** A **5% commission** on all peer-to-peer escrow payments for breeding fees and puppy sales.
* **Partnerships & Advertising:** Collaborative revenue from pet stores, veterinary clinics, and professional breeders. 
* **Event Monetization:** Ticket sales for community-driven dog meetups and professional showcases.
