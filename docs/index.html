What Woofer does

Everything your dog's care needs, in one place

🔬

AI Breed Scanner

Upload a photo and our MobileNetV2 neural network instantly identifies the breed with confidence scoring and detailed characteristic breakdown.

📋

Personalized Care Plans

Powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation, get breed-specific nutrition, exercise, grooming, and health recommendations tailored to your dog.

📄

Care Guide PDFs

Download beautiful, printable care guides to share with your vet or family. Always have the right information on hand.

🏥

Health Tracker

Monitor vet appointments, exercise goals, grooming schedules, and health milestones — all in one organized dashboard.

🛒

Smart Supply Finder

Ethical affiliate links to food, toys, and accessories through Biopet.az, Wolt, and Tap.az — we never earn from pet sales.

🏠

Adoption Resources

Educational content promoting adoption and responsible ownership. Free premium access for registered shelters and rescue organizations.

How Woofer works

1

2

Built on ethics, not profit from animals 🐾

Woofer never facilitates, promotes, or profits from the sale of animals. We believe technology should serve pet welfare — not commercial breeding markets.
